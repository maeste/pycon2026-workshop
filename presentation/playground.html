The full agent-ready-skill rubric scores a codebase across 8 dimensions, each weighted by how much it affects an AI agent's ability to work autonomously.

For this workshop we remap those 8 dimensions onto 3 axes built around the question an agent silently asks at each stage: understand what to do (INSTRUCT, 38 pts), find where to do it (NAVIGATE, 34 pts), and verify it did it right (VALIDATE, 28 pts). The 3 axes sum to 100.

Your overall score is the raw sum of the points you toggle on. Cross the thresholds 40 / 55 / 70 / 85 / 95 and you climb the maturity ladder L1 to L5.
